Mahshid Khalaj

Mahshid Khalaj, a multidisciplinary artist and a graduate in Painting, with an extensive background in graphic design, animation, and motion graphics, began her practice in Generative Art and live audio-visual performance in 2022. She founded the Error-403 platform as a space for creating and presenting interactive and immersive works, and has since produced and performed innovative projects that explore the intersection of image and sound. Her works are grounded in the creation of interactive multimedia experiences, utilizing generative art techniques and audio-reactive systems to blur the boundaries between image and sound within the context of live performance, integrating visual and auditory data to construct immersive and experiential environments.
